# Module 6: Skill Validation (Purple Fusion)

> *Validate full purple team capability under time pressure.*

## Overview

The Skill Validation module provides certification-track simulation and purple team scoring.

## Sanctuary (FUSION) Interface

- **Certification engine**: PNPT/OSCP/CWSP track simulation with timed challenges
- **Purple Team scoring**: Points for exploit (+10), detect (+10), harden (+10), document (+5). Pure offense capped at 30%.
- **Scenario library**: Pre-built campaigns with progressive difficulty
- **Community playbook repository**: Users publish detection rules, credited and rated by community

## Scoring System

| Deliverable | Points | Max % of Total |
|------------|--------|----------------|
| Exploit (Red) | +10 per technique | 30% |
| Detect (Blue) | +10 per rule | 30% |
| Harden (Blue) | +10 per remediation | 25% |
| Document (Both) | +5 per finding | 15% |

**Pure offense is capped at 30%.** Purple team capability requires both offensive and defensive deliverables.

## Certification Tracks

| Track | Duration | Focus | Prerequisites |
|-------|----------|-------|---------------|
| PNPT Simulation | 24 hours | External + internal, report writing | Network fundamentals |
| OSCP Simulation | 48 hours | Multiple targets, full kill chain | Pentest fundamentals |
| CWSP Simulation | 12 hours | Wireless-focused, RF analysis | Wireless fundamentals |
| Purple Team | 72 hours | Full co-evolution cycle | Red + Blue completion |

## Training Objective

Validate full purple team capability under time pressure with both offensive and defensive deliverables required for certification.
